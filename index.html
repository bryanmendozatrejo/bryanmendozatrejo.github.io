About Me

I’m a Data Scientist at Panasonic Avionics, working at the intersection of data science and data engineering. I build and maintain ETL pipelines and analytics workflows using Python and AWS (Glue, Athena, Lambda, QuickSight) to turn raw aviation data into reliable, usable insights. My work has increasingly focused on production data systems—improving pipeline reliability, ensuring data quality, and supporting operational workloads—which has naturally led me towards the data engineering path. I hold a Bachelor’s in Computer Science from Cal State Fullerton and am currently pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science with an emphasis in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. I’m passionate about building new technologies to solving real-world problems.

Feel free to connect if you’d like to talk about data engineering or learn more about my journey!
