TapTapTheButton

A simple tapping game.

How to play!

TapTapTheButton is an easy to play tapping game. The goal is simple try to tap the button on screen without running out of time. Each successful tap will increase your score and time.

Warning: tapping the background instead of the button will result in an instant loss!
